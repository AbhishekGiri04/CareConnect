# Voice Commands Guide

## Supported Voice Commands

### Individual LED Control
- **"Turn on LED 1"** or **"Turn on light 1"**
- **"Turn off LED 1"** or **"Turn off light 1"**
- **"Turn on LED 2"** or **"Turn on light 2"**
- **"Turn off LED 2"** or **"Turn off light 2"**
- **"Turn on LED 3"** or **"Turn on light 3"**
- **"Turn off LED 3"** or **"Turn off light 3"**
- **"Turn on LED 4"** or **"Turn on light 4"**
- **"Turn off LED 4"** or **"Turn off light 4"**

### All Lights Control
- **"Turn on all lights"** or **"All lights on"**
- **"Turn off all lights"** or **"All lights off"**

### Alternative Phrases
You can also use:
- "LED one", "LED two", "LED three", "LED four"
- "Light one", "Light two", "Light three", "Light four"
